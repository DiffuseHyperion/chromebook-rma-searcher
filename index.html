DiffuseHyperion's Chromebook RMA Shim Downloader

This helps you search known hosts for raw RMA shims and built shimmer images for your own chromebook board.

Currently, only 2 hosts are included.

I will try my best to ensure all files provided are working, but I give no guarantees!

These files are not provided, sourced nor supported by Mercury Workshop!


As of 14/2/2024, my hosted shims are incredibly dated. I highly recommend you use Darkn's shims for the time being.

If you downloaded a raw RMA shim, you may inject sh1mmer with this builder.
